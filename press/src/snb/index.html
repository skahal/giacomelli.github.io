Description

Play the next generation of the classic game, controlling the most destructive power of the seas with a finger touch.

History

Ships N’ Battles is a easy game to learn, but with all the bonus combinations and strategy, you’ll need to learn to use the tools to succeed and crash your enemies (especially that friend of yours that keep winning). Multiplayer Bored to always beat the machine? Brings your friends to the battle field! Ships N’ Battles offer a multiplayer mode via Game Center, bluetooth, Lan e Global Server! Who will be the king of the seas? With Global Server technology, Ships N’ Battles allows you to play multiplayer cross-platform. In other words, you have a iPhone and do you want to play against your friend that has a Android? No problem, just connect by Global Server! Bonuses With 10 different bonuses, Ships N’ Battles is a more dynamic game then others. Compare your rating in the World Ranking leaderboard and show who is the king of the seas. Earn trophies, more than 30 achivements to unlock!

Features

  • 4 types of ships
  • 10 different bonuses
  • 4 levels of difficulty
  • Multiplayer via Global Server (play against others iOS, Mac and Android)
  • Multiplayer via bluetooth (iOS)
  • Multiplayer via LAN (wifi)
  • Accelerated mode in the single player

About Skahal Studios

Boilerplate
The Skahal Studios is an indie game development company. Created in 2011 by Diego Giacomelli and Giusepe Casagrande, they start their journey with Ships N' Battles, a revamped battleship game with multiplayer between iOS, MacOs and Android devices.
